
## Fount Mobile: The Native Android Core

The next major chapter in the Fount story is a native Android experience. It isn't just a port; it’s a reimagining of the "Zen" writing flow for a touch-first world.

- **The "Invisible" Editor**: When you open Fount on your phone, the app disappears. The experience is purely about the script. No toolbars, no distractions.
- **The Heart of the Typewriter**: We’re solving the mobile-writer’s biggest pain. The cursor stays **dead-center**, and the editor scrolls your script automatically so you never have to type at the bottom of a cramped screen.
- **The Structural Swipe**: A clean, native drawer that pulls in from the left to reveal your "Structural Thread." One tap and you’re instantly transported to any section or scene.
- **Purely Portable**: A tool for your `.fountain` files. Save them locally, export to a professional PDF on the go, and keep your creativity fluid.
