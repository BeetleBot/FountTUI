# Privacy Policy for Fount

Fount is a terminal-based Fountain screenplay editor. We value your privacy and do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data.

## Data Collection
Fount operates entirely locally on your machine. It does not collect any personal data, email addresses, or usage statistics. Your screenplays and files remain on your local system and are never uploaded to any servers by this application.

## Third Parties
Fount does not share any data with third parties.

## Security
Since no data is collected or transmitted, your scripts and personal information remain entirely on your local system, protected by your own system's security.

## Contact
If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact the developer via the official GitHub repository.
